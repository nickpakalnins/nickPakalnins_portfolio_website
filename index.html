Hello! My name is Nick Pakalnins. I’m currently 30 years old and I reside in Hartland Michigan. I grew up playing hockey for 15 years and golf for 10 years. Logistics is a fantastic field to be in, but my passion was always computers. This led me to continue my education and enroll as a full time student at Oakland University to obtain a Bachelor’s Degree in Information Technology, with a professional track in Cybersecurity.
